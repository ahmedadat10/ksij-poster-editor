🎨 Quick Actions

💡 How to Use

  1. Click any text in the poster to edit it
  2. Change dates, times, programmes, hadith
  3. For Shahadat: Right-click notice → Change to Black
  4. For Wiladat: Right-click notice → Change to Red
  5. Click Download PNG when done!

🎨 Theme Helpers

Right-click on any notice box to change its theme:

✅ Regular (Green)
⚫ Shahadat (Black)
🔴 Wiladat (Red)
