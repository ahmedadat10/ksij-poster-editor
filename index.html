🎨 Quick Actions

💡 How to Use

  1. Click any text to edit it
  2. Drag days to reorder them (grab and move)
  3. Click + Add Prayer Row to add more prayers
  4. Click + Add Programme to show event details
  5. Click + Add Notice to add notice boxes
  6. Right-click colored boxes to change themes
  7. Click Download PNG when done!

🎨 Theme Helpers

Right-click on any colored box to change theme:

✅ Regular (Green)
⚫ Shahadat (Black)
🔴 Wiladat (Red)

↕️ Reorder Days

Click and drag any day section to move it up or down. Perfect for rearranging the week!
