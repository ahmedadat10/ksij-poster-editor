🕌 KSIJ Poster Editor

🎨 Quick Actions

💡 How to Use

  1. Tap any text to edit it
  2. Drag days to reorder (press & hold)
  3. Tap + Add Day to add more days
  4. Hover/tap 🗑️ Delete Day to remove days
  5. Tap + Add Prayer for more prayer times
  6. Use theme dropdown to change colors
  7. Tap Download PNG when done!

🎨 Themes

Use dropdown on each section:

✅ Regular (Green)
⚫ Shahadat (Black)
🔴 Wiladat (Red)

📱 Mobile Tips

• Scroll poster left/right to see all content
• Pinch to zoom if needed
• Download saves to your phone
• Works best in landscape mode
